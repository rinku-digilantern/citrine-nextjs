Faqs About Melasma & Pigmentation
1. What is pigmentation and how can it be treated? −
Pigmentation occurs when melanin production in the skin becomes uneven, leading to dark spots, patches, or discoloration. At Citrine Clinic Gurgaon, advanced pigmentation treatments are offered using chemical peels, lasers, and medical-grade skincare to restore even-toned, radiant skin safely and effectively.
2. What is the best treatment for hyperpigmentation? +
The best hyperpigmentation treatment depends on the skin type and cause. Dr. Niti Gaur recommends customized solutions at Citrine Clinic, including laser toning, Cosmelan peel, chemical peels, and topical medications to reduce melasma, freckles, and sun-induced pigmentation for clearer, brighter skin.
3. How does laser treatment help in reducing dark circles? +
Laser treatment for dark circles targets excess pigmentation and improves skin thickness under the eyes. At Citrine Clinic Gurgaon, advanced, FDA-approved lasers are used to lighten under-eye discoloration, boost collagen, and refresh tired-looking eyes with minimal downtime.
4. What are fillers for dark circles and how do they work? +
Under-eye fillers use hyaluronic acid to fill hollows (tear troughs) that create shadowing and make dark circles appear deeper. Fillers for dark circles at Citrine Clinic are provided to restore volume, smooth under-eye contours, and also provide a well-rested, youthful appearance instantly.
5. Are pigmentation and dark circle treatments safe for all skin types? +
Yes. All pigmentation and under-eye treatments at Citrine Clinic are completely safe and tailored for Indian skin. Each patient’s skin type is evaluated before recommending peels, lasers, or fillers to ensure safe, effective, and long-lasting results.

Citrine Clinic is known for its ethical approach, personalized care, and visible results, helping patients achieve brighter, youthful, and even-toned skin.
