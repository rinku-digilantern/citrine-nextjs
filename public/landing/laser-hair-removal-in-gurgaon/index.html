Faqs About Laser Hair Removal

1. Is laser hair removal/reduction permanent? −
Laser permanent hair removal offers long-term hair reduction. While some fine hair may regrow over time, most clients experience up to 80–90% permanent hair reduction after completing the recommended sessions.
2. What is the cost of laser hair removal in Gurgaon?+
Laser hair removal cost in Gurgaon depends on the treatment area (face, underarms, full body) and the number of sessions required. At Citrine Clinic, we offer affordable laser hair removal packages with customized plans for every client.
3. How many sessions are required for laser hair removal? +
Typically, 6–8 sessions are required for optimal results. However, this may vary based on hair thickness, skin type, and treatment area.
4. Is laser hair removal painful? +
Laser hair removal treatment is mostly painless. You may feel mild tingling or warmth, but advanced cooling technology ensures a comfortable experience.
5. Can women opt for full-body laser hair removal? +
Yes, full-body laser hair removal is one of the most preferred treatments among women for long-term, smooth, hair-free skin across all areas.
6. Are there any side effects of laser hair removal? +
Laser hair removal is generally safe with minimal side effects like slight redness or sensitivity, which usually subsides within a few hours.
7. Is underarm laser hair removal effective? +
Yes, underarm laser hair removal is highly effective and one of the quickest treatments, helping reduce hair growth, pigmentation, and ingrown hair issues.
8. Is there a permanent solution for uneven beard lines? +
Yes, laser beard shaping offers a long-term solution for uneven beard lines by removing unwanted hair on the cheeks and neck, giving you a cleaner, well-defined beard with minimal maintenance.
