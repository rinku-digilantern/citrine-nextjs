Faqs About Hair Treatment

1. What is GFC hair treatment and how does it work? −
GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate) hair treatment is an advanced, non-surgical procedure that uses your own blood’s growth factors to stimulate hair follicles, reduce hair fall, and promote new hair growth. At Citrine Clinic Gurgaon, Dr. Niti Gaur performs GFC treatment using a safe, FDA-approved process with minimal downtime.
2. What is exosome therapy for hair loss? +
Exosome therapy is a next-generation hair restoration treatment that delivers powerful growth factors and stem-cell-derived exosomes to rejuvenate weak hair follicles. Exosome hair therapy at Citrine Clinic Gurgaon is provided to improve hair density, thickness, and scalp health naturally.
3. Which is better for hair loss — GFC or exosome therapy? +
Both GFC and exosome therapy are highly effective, and Dr. Niti Gaur recommends the best option based on the cause and severity of your hair loss. GFC uses your body’s own plasma growth factors, while exosomes work at a cellular level to enhance regeneration — both stimulate strong, healthy hair growth.
4. How many sessions are needed for visible hair growth results? +
Typically, 4–6 sessions of GFC or exosome therapy spaced 4–6 weeks apart are recommended for noticeable improvement in hair density and volume. At Citrine Clinic Gurgaon, the treatment plan is customized depending on individual scalp condition and response rate.
