Faqs About Facials in Gurgaon

1. What is the difference between a salon facial and a dermatologist-designed medifacial? −
Salon facials are cosmetic and surface-level, while medifacials use medical-grade products and technologies tailored to your skin condition. They offer deeper, longer-lasting results.
2. How do I know which facial is right for my skin? +
A dermatologist assesses your skin concerns—acne, dullness, pigmentation, tanning, or sensitivity to recommend the most effective facial. You’ll get a personalised plan based on what your skin actually needs.
3. Are the results instantly visible after the treatment? +
Most facials like Hydrafacial and Medifacial show immediate glow, hydration, and smoothness. Laser-based facials improve clarity and tone within a few days as your skin regenerates.
4. Is there any downtime after carbon facial, photofacial, or laser toning? +
These treatments usually have little to no downtime; you may experience mild redness for a few hours. Most clients return to work the same day.
5. Can I do makeup immediately after a facial? +
It’s best to avoid makeup for at least 12–24 hours to let your skin breathe. This prevents irritation and ensures better results.
6. Are these facials safe for sensitive skin types? +
Yes, all facials are customised to suit sensitive or reactive skin. Dermatologists choose gentle settings and formulations to avoid irritation.
7. How long does the glow from Hydrafacial or Medifacial last? +
The glow typically lasts 7–10 days, and hydration can last even longer. Regular sessions help maintain long-term skin health and brightness.
8. Do these facials help with anti-ageing or fine lines? +
Yes—treatments like laser toning, photofacials, and medifacials stimulate collagen and improve texture. Over time, they soften fine lines and restore firmness.
9. Is there a combined package for multiple facials or skin concerns? +
Most clinics offer customised packages that combine facials for pigmentation, acne, glow, or anti-ageing. These bundles are more effective and more cost-efficient.
