Faqs About Melasma & Pigmentation

1. What is the cost of melasma treatment in Gurgaon? −

2. What is the best treatment for pigmentation and melasma? +
At Citrine Clinic, pigmentation and melasma are treated using a combination of chemical peels, laser treatments, and medical-grade skincare. Depending on the severity, treatments like Cosmelan Peel, Q-Switched Laser, and Medifacials help fade dark spots, brighten the complexion, and improve overall skin clarity.
3. How long does it take to see results from pigmentation treatment? +
Visible improvement can often be noticed within 3–4 weeks, but optimal results usually require multiple sessions. The number of sessions depends on the type and depth of pigmentation, skin type, and adherence to post-treatment care.
4. Are pigmentation and melasma treatments safe for all skin types? +
Yes, the pigmentation treatments at Citrine Clinic are safe and customized for all Indian skin types. The procedures use advanced technologies and dermatologist-recommended protocols to ensure effective results without damaging the skin.
5. Can pigmentation return after treatment? +
Pigmentation can recur if proper sun protection and skincare maintenance are not followed. Using sunscreen daily, avoiding direct UV exposure, and maintaining a prescribed skincare routine can significantly prolong the results.
6. How many sessions are required for hyperpigmentation treatment? +
Most patients require 4–6 sessions spaced a few weeks apart. But for melasma treatment, the number of sessions required might be higher. The treatment plan is personalized based on the extent and depth of pigmentation.
