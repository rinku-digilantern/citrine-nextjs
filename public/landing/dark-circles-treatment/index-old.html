FAQs on Dark Circle Treatment in Gurgaon

1. What causes dark circles under the eyes? −
Dark circles can result from genetics, lack of sleep, sun exposure, allergies, pigmentation, or volume loss beneath the eyes.
2. Which treatment is best for dark circles?+
The ideal treatment depends on the cause. Lasers work best for pigmentation, fillers for hollowness, and PRP for rejuvenation. A dermatologist will recommend the most effective combination.
3. Are dark circle treatments permanent? +
Results are long-lasting but require maintenance. Proper skincare, hydration, and sun protection help prolong results.
4. How soon will I see results? +
Most patients notice visible improvement after 2–3 sessions, with best results after completing the full treatment plan.
5. Is there any downtime after dark circle treatment? +
Most procedures are non-invasive and have no downtime. You can resume normal activities immediately after treatment.
6. Are the treatments safe for all skin types? +
Yes. Citrine Clinic uses advanced devices and dermatologist-tested techniques that are safe for Indian skin types.
7. Can home remedies or creams treat dark circles? +
Topical creams may help lighten mild pigmentation, but deeper or vascular dark circles need professional treatments for effective correction.
